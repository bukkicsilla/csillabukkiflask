#We randomly assigned 50 patients to the treatment group and 50 patients to the control group. 
#Both groups are given a new drug to treat high cholesterol. 
#In both groups the mean is 250, and the standard deviation is 25.
#We compare the mean cholesterol in the two groups by using the 2 sample t test (independent samples). 
#The simulation is done 10,000 times, so there are 10,000 p-values.

    control = np.random.normal(250, 25, 50)

#The standard deviation is 25 for both groups.

#Histogram of the p-values does not look uniform. There is a real  effect here and we see a right-skewed distribution.
